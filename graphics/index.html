Bob-omb Raytracing

Objective 1-3

I added the cylinder and torus primitives, implemented constructive solid geometry, and modeled the bob-ombs using the combination of those.

The eyes are toruses intersected with the main body.

The fuse is a torus with cubes differenced to cut off parts of it.

The grey blasting cap is a cylinder intersected with a cone differenced with another cylinder at the top of the cone.

The wind up key is two cylinders unioned with a cube, with two smaller cylinders differenced inside.
